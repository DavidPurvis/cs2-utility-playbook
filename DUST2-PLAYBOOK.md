# Dust 2 Playbook

Scenario-based utility executes for coordinated CS2 squads. Designed for 2–3 active players in a 5-stack; the remaining 1–2 teammates freelance or hold elsewhere.

Player slots: **A** (blue), **B** (amber), **C** (green).

---

## A Site

### A Long Execute · 2 players
Standard 2-man Long take. Fast, repeatable, hard to stop without rotating CTs early.

- **Player A — Long Support / Smoker**
  1. Out of T Spawn, jump-throw **Xbox smoke**.
  2. Walk through mid doors to Long Corner.
  3. On entry call, throw **A Cross smoke** (left-click from Long Corner).
  4. Trade your entry on site.
- **Player B — Entry / Flash**
  1. Rush Long with your entry.
  2. Pop **A Long entry flash** from Long Corner as Xbox smoke pops.
  3. Once A Cross smoke is up, throw **A Car molly**.
  4. Swing onto site through default — count on Player A to trade.

### A Full Execute · 3 players
Long + Short pinch onto A. The most complete A take on the map.

- **Player A — Long Support / Smoker** — Xbox smoke, A Cross smoke. Push to Long Corner; trade Long entry.
- **Player B — Long Entry** — A Long entry flash, A Car molly. Rush Long; pop flash; molly Car; enter on cross smoke.
- **Player C — Short Entry** — A Stairs smoke, A Short push flash, Goose molly. Push Catwalk; smoke Stairs; flash and swing Short; Goose molly post-clear.

### A Short / Cat Execute · 2 players
When Long is locked down by an AWP, hit A through Short with 2 players.

- **Player A — Short Support / Smoker** — Xbox smoke from T Spawn; push to Catwalk; A Stairs smoke; trade Player B's swing.
- **Player B — Short Entry** — Push Catwalk; A Short push flash as Stairs smoke goes up; swing Short; Goose molly to flush the hidden angle.

## B Site

### B Tunnels Rush · 2 players
Fast 2-man B take when CTs don't rotate. Hits the site within 25 seconds.

- **Player A — Tuns Support / Smoker** — Take Upper Tunnels; B Window smoke, B Doors smoke (jump-throw); trade Player B's entry.
- **Player B — B Entry** — Move with A to Upper Tuns; B entry flash as Window smoke connects; swing site through Doors; B Car molly to flush the back hold.

### B Full Execute · 3 players
3-man B take with full utility coverage. Smokes seal the angles, mollies pre-flush plant and car, flash for the swing.

- **Player A — Smoker** — Upper Tuns; B Window smoke, B Doors smoke; trade entry; hold default after plant.
- **Player B — Entry / Flash** — B Entry flash, B Default molly; swing site.
- **Player C — Anti-Hold Molly** — B Car molly to flush the back corner; swing third; hold back-site post-plant.

### B Mid Split · 3 players
Two players hit B from Tunnels; the third flanks through Mid → CT Mid → B Doors after CT Mid smoke. Maximum chaos for a B retake call.

- **Player A — Tuns Smoker** — Take Upper Tuns; B Window smoke; B Entry flash as "mid through" is called; swing site.
- **Player B — Tuns Entry** — B Doors smoke; B Car molly; entry swing site with flash.
- **Player C — Mid Flank** — Xbox smoke; push mid, throw CT Mid smoke; take mid doors, push to CT Mid; call "mid through" and swing B Doors as Player A entries.

## Mid Control

### Mid Control · 2 players
Map control opener for default rounds. Enables a late rotation to either site once you read the CT setup.

- **Player A — Mid Smoker** — Xbox smoke; push mid doors; CT Mid smoke; hold mid-doors while B takes Catwalk.
- **Player B — Cat Control** — Move with A through mid; take Catwalk under their CT Mid smoke; pop Short flash to clear cat info; hold cat or drop to short for a late-round split.

## Lineup catalog

The 17 lineups referenced above. Coordinates are CS2 world coords; throw screenshots live in the app.

### T-side · Essential smokes
- **Xbox Smoke** — T Spawn → Xbox · Jump throw · Cuts mid-doors AWP angle.
- **A Cross Smoke** — Long Corner → A Cross · Left click · Blocks CT→A crossing sightline.
- **CT Spawn Smoke from Long** — A Long → CT Spawn · Jump throw · Cuts CT rotates and info-peeks.
- **B Doors Smoke** — Upper Tunnels → B Doors · Jump throw · Blocks CT rotates and B holds from doors.
- **B Window Smoke** — Upper Tunnels → B Window · Left click · Blocks the sniper window angle.

### T-side · Important smokes
- **A Car Smoke** — Long → A Car · Jump throw · Blocks the car-CT angle for post-plant.
- **A Stairs Smoke** — Catwalk → A Short · Left click · Stops short/cat defenders from holding stairs.
- **CT Mid Smoke** — T Spawn → CT Mid · Run + jump throw · Cuts CT-mid pickers for B splits.

### T-side · Essential flashes
- **A Long Entry Flash** — Long Corner → A Long · Jump throw · Pop flash for the long take.
- **B Entry Flash** — Upper Tunnels → B Site · Jump throw · Blinds B defenders for tunnels entries.
- **A Short Push Flash** — Catwalk → A Short · Left click · Flashes A Short / Goose for short entry.

### T-side · Important molotovs
- **A Car Molly** — Long → A Car · Left click · Forces AWPer off the car corner.
- **B Default Plant Molly** — Upper Tunnels → B Default · Jump throw · Pre-flushes the default plant.
- **B Car Molly** — Upper Tunnels → B Car · Left click · Flushes the back-of-B-car corner.
- **Goose Molly** — Long → A Goose · Jump throw · Clears the Goose off-angle anchor spot.

### CT-side · Retake & anti-execute
- **B Retake Flash from Scaffolding** — B Scaffolding → B Site · Left click · Pops over B for retake swings.
- **B Plant Molly from CT/Window** — B Window → B Default · Left click · Drops on plant during retake.

## How to use

1. Pick the **target zone** on the radar (where you want utility to land).
2. Read the **scenarios** for that site. Pick one that matches your team's count.
3. In the scenario view, click **"I'm A/B/C"** to highlight your role.
4. Export the scenario as JSON to share with teammates (clipboard).
